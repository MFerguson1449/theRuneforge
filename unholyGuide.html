Overview

Unholy Death Knight currently provides moderate sustainable single target damage, as well as extremely potent AoE damage in both burst and sustain situations. Unholy is particularly powerful against 4+ targets.

Strengths

  • - Strong single target, AoE, and cleave DPS (varies with talent choice)
  • - High initial single target burst (First 30-40 seconds of encounter)
  • - Powerful damage mitigation (Including a spell immunity)
  • - Strong ulitity (in the form of Death Grip, Mind Freeze, Asphyxiate, and Gnaw

Weaknesses

  • - Poor target swapping due to Festering Wounds
  • - Very low mobility
  • - Small ramp up time with Festering Wounds, and numerous cooldowns

Talents

Many talent combinations will work just fine, these are just recommendations for day to day gameplay. These talents should be adjusted as needed to properly utilize the utility that the Death Knight class provides.

Bursting Sores plays a large part in what makes Unholy Death Knights thrive in 4+ target situations, mastering the application and explosion of Festering Wounds is integral in mastering the Unholy Death Knight, especially in AoE situations. We will talk more about this later on in the rotation section.

Level 56 Talents

Infected Claws - Causes your ghoul’s Claw ability to have a 30% chance on hit to apply a stack of Festering Wound.

All Will Serve - Makes your Raise Dead now summon a second, weaker, minion.

Clawing Shadows - Gives your Scourge Strike a 30 yard range, and causes it to deal shadow damage, as opposed to Scourge Strike which is part-shadow, part physical.

Level 57 Talents

Bursting Sores - Causes Festering Wound to deal 25% more damage when burst, and also causes it to deal damage to nearby enemies.

Ebon Fever - Passively increases the damage of Virulent Plague by 15%, while also halving it’s duration.

Unholy Blight - (30 second cooldown) Grants a 6 second buff that applies a disease to nearby enemies which lasts 14 seconds.

Level 58 Talents

Grip of the Dead - Causes Death and Decay to slow all enemies within it, reducing by 10% per second for it’s duration.

Death’s Reach - Increasese the range of Death Grip by 10 yards, and causes it’s cooldown to reset upon killing an enemy.

Asphyxiate - (45 second cooldown - 20 yard range) Stuns your target for 4 seconds.

Level 60 Talents

Pestilent Pustules - Bursting a Festering Wound has 10% chance to grant Runic Corruption (a buff that increases your Rune generation rate by 100% for 3 seconds)

Harbinger of Doom - Causes Sudden Doom to proc 15% more often, and can now hold 2 charges.

Soul Reaper - (45 second cooldown) Deals Shadow damage over 8 seconds and generates 2 Runes. If the target dies during it’s duration, it also grants an 8 second buff increasing your haste by 10%.

Level 75 Talents

Spell Eater - Increases the duration of Anti-Magic Shell by 5 seconds, and increases it’s absorption capacity by 30%.

Wraith Walk - (1 minute cooldown - Channeled) Increases your movement speed by 70%, and prevents your movement speed from being reduced below %170.

Death Pact - (2 minute cooldown) Heals you for 50% of your maximum health, but also grants you a debuff absorbing healing equal to 30% of your maximum health for 15 seconds.

Level 90 Talents

Pestilence - Makes your Death and Decay have a 10% chance to apply a stack of Festering Wound to any enemy when it deals damage.

Defile - (20 second cooldown - replaces Death and Decay) Upon every tick, it’s size increases if there are enemies standing within it. Scourge Strike still cleaves nearby targets while you are standing within your own Defile.

Epidemic - (Costs 30 runic power) Causes all targets afflicted by your Virulent Plague to explode dealing Shadow damage to the affected target, and smaller Shadow damage to all nearby enemies.

Level 100 Talents

Army of the Damned - Makes your Death Coil and Epidemic casts reduce the cooldown of Apocalypse by second and Army of the Dead by 5 seconds.

Unholy Frenzy - (1 minute 30 second cooldown) Grants a 20% haste buff and makes your auto attacks have a chance to apply Festering Wound to the target for 12 seconds.

Summon Gargoyle - (3 minute cooldown) Summons a Gargoyle that lasts for 30 seconds. The Gargoyle gains 1% increased damage for every 2 Runic Power you spend for it’s duration.
