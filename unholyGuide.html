Legendaries

Single Target

  • - Available Item Slots: Chest, Back
  • - Recommended Secondaries: Mastery & Haste

Koltiras Favor is the best legendary for Single Target situations for both builds. It offers a bit of a RNG factor to Breath of Sindragosa and can make for some very long breaths when it procs often. It also synergizes very well with Obliteration builds making this the best legendary for Single Target all around.

M+ / AoE

  • - Available Item Slots: Legs, Back
  • - Recommended Secondaries: Mastery & Haste

Biting Cold is a very powerful legendary for AoE and cleave situations. The damage increase to Remorseless Winter, and free Rime procs are substantial enough to make this the most powerful pick for Mythic+.

Stat Weights

Strength is the most important stat for Frost Death Knight, because of this more often than not iLevel will be the deciding factor in determining an upgrade. Following Strength you will want to be looking for Mastery with the rest of the stats being someone equal.

For a general template:

Strength > Mastery > Haste > Critical Strike = Versatility


Keep in mind it is always recommended to sim any items to determine an upgrade.
