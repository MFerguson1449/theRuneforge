Overview

Frost is a melee speculation for the Death Knight Class. Frost has a high burst potential and excels in both single target and grouped cleave situations. We have a few effective defensive abilities in the form of Icebound Fortitude, Anti-Magic Shell, and following the release of Shadowlands Anti-Magic Zone.

Strengths

  • - Strong defensive cooldowns
  • - High burst damage & cleave with Breath of Sindragosa
  • - Strong ulitity (in the form of Death Grip, Mind Freeze, Asphyxiate, and Anti-Magic Zone

Weaknesses

  • - Limited spread target cleave.
  • - Very low mobility
  • - The Breath of Sindragosa build tends to be a very cooldown dependant.

Level 15 Talents

Inexorable Assault - A passive talent granting a stack every 8 seconds, up to 5 stacks. Using Obliterate consumes one stack dealing extra Frost Damage.

Icy Talons - Using Frost Strike, Breath of Sindragosa, and Glacial Advance grants a stack increasing your attack speed by 5% for 6 seconds, up to 3 stacks. The attack speed buff stacks, but the buff duration does not go passed 6 seconds.

Cold Heart - A passive talent granting a stack of Cold Heart every 2 seconds, up to 20 stacks. When you cast Chains of Ice your stacks are consumed dealing extra Frost Damage.

Level 25 Talents

Runic Attenuation - Makes your auto attacks have a chance to generate 5 Runic Power.

Murderous Efficiency - Each time you consume a Killing Machine you have a 50% chance to gain 1 rune.

Horn of Winter - An activatable ability that grants you 2 runes and 25 runic power on use.

Level 30 Talents

Death’s Reach - Increasese the range of Death Grip by 10 yards, and causes it’s cooldown to reset upon killing an enemy.

Asphyxiate - (45 second cooldown - 20 yard range) Stuns your current target for 4 seconds.

Blinding Sleet - (1 minute cooldown) Disorients all targets in a cone in front of you for 5 seconds, when the disorient ends all targets affected are slowed by 50% for 6 seconds.

Level 35 Talents

Avalanche - All Howling Blast casts that are empowered by Rime also deal extra Frost Damage to the target.

Frozen Pulse - Causes all auto attacks to deal frost damage to nearby enemies when you have fewer than 3 full runes.

Frostscythe - An active ability that cleaves up to 5 targets in front of you, costing 1 rune. This ability also benefits from Killing Machine. Additionally this ability does 4x damage on critical strikes.

Level 40 Talents

Spell Eater - Increases the duration of Anti-Magic Shell by 5 seconds, and increases it’s absorption capacity by 30%.

Wraith Walk - (1 minute cooldown - Channeled) Increases your movement speed by 70%, and prevents your movement speed from being reduced below %170.

Death Pact - (2 minute cooldown) Heals you for 50% of your maximum health, but also grants you a debuff absorbing healing equal to 30% of your maximum health for 15 seconds.

Level 90 Talents

Pestilence - Makes your Death and Decay have a 10% chance to apply a stack of Festering Wound to any enemy when it deals damage.

Defile - (20 second cooldown - replaces Death and Decay) Upon every tick, it’s size increases if there are enemies standing within it. Scourge Strike still cleaves nearby targets while you are standing within your own Defile.

Epidemic - (Costs 30 runic power) Causes all targets afflicted by your Virulent Plague to explode dealing Shadow damage to the affected target, and smaller Shadow damage to all nearby enemies.

Level 100 Talents

Army of the Damned - Makes your Death Coil and Epidemic casts reduce the cooldown of Apocalypse by second and Army of the Dead by 5 seconds.

Unholy Frenzy - (1 minute 30 second cooldown) Grants a 20% haste buff and makes your auto attacks have a chance to apply Festering Wound to the target for 12 seconds.

Summon Gargoyle - (3 minute cooldown) Summons a Gargoyle that lasts for 30 seconds. The Gargoyle gains 1% increased damage for every 2 Runic Power you spend for it’s duration.
